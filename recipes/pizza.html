Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meat, etc., which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.
